Information security/信息安全

Safety measures：

Our working-flow strictly abides by the standard system of the ISO27001 information security management system.We guarantee customers that their information is absolutely secure with us by applying such working-flow and security system.

Specific measures：

Separated offices for administration department and project department

Enterprise level hardware firewall

24-hour monitor

Electronic devices are forbidden in working area

Confidential agreement signed by every engineer

Strict registration system on access

Independent network for FTP server                                                        

Independent local area network for project department

Separated room for data server

Training on information security for the whole staff

Specific control on data transfer

seal off USB ports of equipment
